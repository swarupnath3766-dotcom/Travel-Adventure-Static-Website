  • Nepal

Scuba Diving

Scuba diving offers a mix of physical and mental wellness. It provides a full-body cardiovascular workout, burns up to 600 calories per hour, and improves flexibility. The sport's mandatory deep, mindful breathing lowers heart rate and stress, while exploring marine life builds a strong connection to ocean conservation.

The Best Countries for Scuba Diving Around the World are:

  • Indonesia
  • Egypt (The Red Sea)
  • The Maldives
  • Mexico
  • Japan
